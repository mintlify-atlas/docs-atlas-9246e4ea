      ```json

        "language": "en",
        "license": "CC BY"
      }
      ```
    </Step>
    <Step title="Add content">
      Import existing content from the public library, upload your own files (videos, PDFs, exercises), or use ricecooker to programmatically import content at scale.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Publish to Kolibri">
      When your channel is ready, click **Publish** to make it available for import into Kolibri instances. Your content will be packaged and distributed for offline learning.
